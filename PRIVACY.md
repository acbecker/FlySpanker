# Privacy Policy for FlySpanker

**Last updated: December 28, 2025**

## Overview

FlySpanker is designed with your privacy in mind. The app processes all images entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No photos or images are uploaded to any server
- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No cookies or similar technologies
- No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs

## Photo Access

FlySpanker requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to select images for comparison. Selected photos are processed entirely on your device and are never transmitted elsewhere.

## Data Storage

All app data remains on your device. FlySpanker does not use any cloud services or external servers.

## Third-Party Services

FlySpanker does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

FlySpanker does not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/acbecker/FlySpanker/issues) on our GitHub repository.
